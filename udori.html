Culture Discovery for would-be employees.

If culture fit is a priority in your company then cultured is for you. Cultured is an app that allows you curate stories about your culture, so perspective employees can understand your culture before they apply.

Curate stories about your culture.

With cultured, you can document stories about culture in an easy and interesting way. From showing exposes on your office space to giving insights on how you brainstorm. Cultured lets you provide an immersive experience for people interested in your company.

A way to show your perks & benefits

As the right candidates look for jobs, they see employee benefits as an important factor. With cultured, you can show the benefits your employees enjoy in an engaging manner.

A way to show your events and conferences

With cultured, you can show your upcoming events and get users to subscribe for recaps-stories of the event.

Why Cultured?

Hiring for culture-fit isnt a myth. 80% of employees want to know a company's culture before they get in. With cultured you can attract the best employees.

Sometimes company's organize internal events & dont preserve memories.With cultured, you can keep persistent workday memories for your employees.
